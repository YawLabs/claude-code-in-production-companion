# Recording

Production plans for any video sessions that accompany the book. Author-facing notes -- shot lists, screen-capture cues, retake reasons, equipment notes. Not part of the book content; published here for transparency about how the materials were made.

```
recording/
+-- module-1-plan.md
+-- module-2-plan.md
+-- module-3-plan.md
+-- module-4-plan.md
```

Currently scaffolded -- the per-module plans land as each video is produced.

If you're considering producing your own technical content alongside a book, these plans may be useful as templates. The format is opinionated and worked for me; your shape may vary.
