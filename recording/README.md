# Recording

Production plans for the video sessions. These are author-facing notes -- shot lists, screen-capture cues, retake reasons, equipment notes. Not part of the course content; published here for transparency about how the materials were made.

```
recording/
+-- module-1-plan.md
+-- module-2-plan.md
+-- module-3-plan.md
+-- module-4-plan.md
```

Currently scaffolded -- the per-module plans land as each video is produced.

If you're considering producing your own technical course, these plans may be useful as templates. The format is opinionated and worked for me; your shape may vary.
